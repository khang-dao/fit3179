Australia Olympic Medals Over the Years

The year 2000 was Australia's best performance in terms of Olympic medals. We won 25 gold medals, 16 silver medals, and 17 bronze medals. Could this have anything to do with it being hosted in Sydney? Sadly, our performance decreased in the years following up until Tokyo 2020.
